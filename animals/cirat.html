Fast Facts

Species: Christmas Island Rat

Extinct: Early 1900s

Habitat: Christmas Island (Tropical Forests)

About

The Christmas Island rat was a native rodent that went extinct due to disease introduced by invasive species. Scientists are exploring gene-editing techniques to recreate it using closely related rat species.
