Fast Facts

Species: Dire Wolf (Aenocyon dirus)

Extinct: ~10,000 years ago

Habitat: North & South America

About

The dire wolf was a powerful apex predator of the Ice Age, known for its massive build and strong jaws. Unlike modern wolves, it was bulkier and specialized in hunting large prey like bison and horses. Scientists are exploring de-extinction using preserved DNA and modern wolf relatives, potentially bringing back one of the most iconic prehistoric predators.
