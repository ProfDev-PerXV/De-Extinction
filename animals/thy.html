Fast Facts

Species: Tasmanian Tiger (Thylacine)

Extinct: 1936

Habitat: Tasmania, Australia

About

The thylacine was a carnivorous marsupial often called the Tasmanian tiger due to its striped back. It went extinct after human hunting. Scientists are attempting to revive it using DNA and its closest relative, the numbat.
