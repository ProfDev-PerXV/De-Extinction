Fast Facts

Species: Dodo

Extinct: Late 1600s

Habitat: Mauritius Island

About

The dodo was a flightless bird native to Mauritius. It became extinct due to human hunting and introduced animals. Scientists aim to bring it back using DNA from preserved remains and its closest relatives like pigeons.
