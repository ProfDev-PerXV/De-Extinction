Fast Facts

Species: Woolly Mammoth

Extinct: ~4,000 Years Ago

Habitat: Arctic Tundra

About

The woolly mammoth was a large, cold-adapted elephant species that lived during the Ice Age. Scientists are working to bring it back by editing the DNA of Asian elephants to create a cold-resistant hybrid.
